¶ Extended Backbone.Wreqr with ability to cancel trigger.

Most apps depend heavely on triggers. There are times where you would like to stop a trigger from happening. Here the handy beforeTrigger comes in.

If this is defined as a function, and it returns false, then that event won't be triggered.
